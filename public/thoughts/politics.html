Fuck! The! Governement!

The only reason why governments have power is because they declared that they do and killed anyone who said otherwise. They are not a natural thing. They are not a fact of life. They are not necessary. If everyone woke up tomorrow and decided that governmental structures needed to go, collectively we could make it happen. There is nothing stopping us. Humans existed for hundreds and thousands of years without government just fine until uh oh a select few got power hungry and now we have capitalism and what should not be called democracy.

The thing is though, that most people don't think that and they do actually have complaints against capitalism but the second you bring up how what they're upset at is capitalism suddenly you're being stupid and don't understand how the world works. Like look me in the eye and tell me that capitalism is a successful system.
